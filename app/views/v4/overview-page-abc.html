What do these terms mean?

Number of employees is the average number of people a company employs during a financial year. It should include people employed by the company under contracts of service.

Balance sheet total shows the value of the company's assets minus its liabilities at the end of its financial year. The company's assets are things the company owns, such as buildings and stock. Liabilities are debts the company owes.

Profit and loss shows how much a company has made from sales minus how much it spent on running costs at the end of its financial year.
